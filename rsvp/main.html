Our Story

How it all began

Two wanderers who found each other in the most unexpected of places. Here is our story, told in the moments that mattered most.

2019
The beginning

A chance encounter at a small bookshop in Florence. James was searching for a Calvino novel; Sofia was reaching for the same shelf. They talked for two hours and forgot about the books entirely.

2020
First adventure

A spontaneous road trip along the Amalfi Coast that was meant to last a weekend and stretched into ten days. They returned with sea glass, terrible sunburns, and the certainty that this was something real.

2022
Home together

They moved into a small apartment in Rome, learned to cook pasta from Sofia's grandmother, and hosted dinners that lasted well past midnight every weekend.

2024
The proposal

At a quiet table at their favourite trattoria — the same one they had stumbled into on that first road trip — James asked Sofia to spend the rest of her life with him. She said yes before he finished the sentence.
